About the Artist

Take an indie–rock band and inject it with a firm attitude and catchy phrases, that's Reality Club for you.

Established in late 2016, Reality Club is an AMI (Anugerah Musik Indonesia) awards winner for Best Alternative Album of 2023 and Best Alternative Group of 2023 (for “Reality Club Presents…”) and nominee for best newcomer of 2018 and best alternative group of 2018, 2020, 2021, and 2022. Formed in Jakarta, they have been ever-growing and catching people's eyes and ears since their first release. They are Era Patigo on drums, Faiz Novascotia Saripudin on vocals and guitar, Fathia Izzati on vocals and keys, and Nugi Wicaksono on bass. They have started their expansion through Asia in 2019, playing in Tokyo, Malaysia, Jeddah, Singapore, and was even scheduled for South By Southwest in Austin, Texas, 2020. They have also recently completed an Online World Tour, playing in online festivals for the UK, Germany, Singapore, and the USA crowd.

Recently, from October through to December of 2023, Reality Club successfully held their third album tour titled "Reality Club Presents... the Asia Tour" which included Singapore, Thailand, Taiwan, Malaysia, Philippines, and a solo concert at Balai Sarbini, Jakarta, Indonesia. This solo concert featured a semi-theatrical concept featuring dancers, a choir, and an orchestra which successfully captivated more than 1,300 audiences for a truly unforgettable night for everyone in attendance and marking a momentous achievement for the band.
